West Marches Campaign

Settlement: Ostenwall

Ostenwall is the capital of (kingdom in Myrellian Concordance?) ruled by duke Rowan Stonehearth II, but is primarily run by a council of nobles.

Ostenwall sits in the middle of a valley among the Venval mountain range with a small patch of forest to the north.

Notable Persons

Duke Rowan Stonehearth II inherited his position from his father 34 years ago. Though he has final say, the Duke allows the city of Ostenwall to be ran by a council of nobles.

Eris Ravenfall is the aggressively self appointed leader of the merchant guilds in Ostenwall. A nice enough woman when you are not in her way, but a shrewd businesswoman. Does not shy away from having someone murdered, hence Cassius' former employment.

Cassius is the former assassin/bodyguard to Eris Ravenfall. He eventually had a change of heart and retired to Thorngate.

Baron Gabriel Archeon: The Now head of the Archeon House and member of the Ostenwall council. Baron Gabriel Archeon is a tall, and well built man, with dark skin and black hair. Winning the favor of lady Eve during his time as a tournament Jouster. He has proven to be a competent military leader, and without his on the fly thinking, would have made the disaster at the battle of Blackrock Pass a total a irrecoverable disaster. While he is a competent, if not great military leader his statecraft is much to be desired, leading him to be seen in the courts as a stiff individual.
